Contact Us

Email Support: Our primary channel for support is through email. Please contact us at info@sos.org.mk. We aim to respond to all queries within 24-48 hours.

Safety and Privacy

The safety and privacy of our users are paramount. We invite you to review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use, accessible within the app and on our official website, for comprehensive details on how we safeguard your data and maintain a secure app environment.

Updates and Notifications

To keep yourself informed about the latest updates, features, and resources, we recommend enabling app notifications and regularly visiting our website or following our social media pages.

Feedback and Suggestions

Your feedback is invaluable in our mission to continuously enhance the Cyberbullying app. If you have any suggestions for new features, resources, or general improvements, please feel free to share them with us via email.
